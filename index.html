Biddlr

Biddlr is an application to create a marketplace ofr workers to complete projects involving unskilled labor at the lowest prices for job posters. Moreover, employers can set a maximum price they will accept before the bidding process begins. Also, job posters can choose not to accept the lowest bid and instead go with a higher bid that has a higher satisfaction rating. In addition, bidders can only bid the prices that they are willing to work for. Furthermore the application will create opportunity to build work credibility for non-professionals. There will be a service fee taken from the job poster, not bidders. Also, biddlr will simplify the searching process by using geographical proximity. In addition, the app will reinforce trust between job poster and job bidder with a rating process.
